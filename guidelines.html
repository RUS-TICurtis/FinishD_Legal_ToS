The FinishD Way

Our guidelines ensure everyone has a supportive environment to achieve their goals.

✓ Encouraged Behavior

  • Goal-Setting: Share your journey and milestones openly.
  • Mutual Support: Celebrate the "Finished" status of your peers.
  • Authenticity: Share real progress, including the struggles along the way.

✗ Prohibited Content

  • Harassment & Bullying: We have zero tolerance for targeted abuse.
  • Hate Speech: Any content attacking individuals based on race, religion, or identity.
  • Misinformation: Sharing fake achievements or misleading health/productivity advice.
  • Illegal Acts: Promoting or sharing any unlawful activities.
